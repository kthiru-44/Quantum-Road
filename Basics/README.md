IBM Basics of Quantum Information 

## 🎓 Course Structure

Completed the course which  covers core foundations in the following order:

1. **Single Systems**
   - Mathematical formalism: quantum state vectors, normalization, unit vectors in ℂⁿ  
   - Projective measurements, probabilities, unitary operations  
   - State preparation and measurement logic for one qubit 

2. **Multiple Systems**
   - Representation of joint systems via tensor products (e.g. 2‑qubit state vectors in 4‑dim space)  
   - Product vs. entangled states, Bell states, partial measurement, and collapse  
   - Correlations between subsystems and their mathematical modeling

3. **Quantum Circuits & Key Protocols**
   - Building circuits using quantum gates (Hadamard, CNOT, etc.)
   - Illustrated protocols: quantum teleportation, superdense coding, and the CHSH game (Bell‑inequality scenario)
